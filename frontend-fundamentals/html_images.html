🥗 Salsa Verde

Great recipe for a sauce that goes well with any protein, especially a nice 🥩 beef steak!

Every steak warrants a sauce and toppings or accompanying side dishes. To cook a nice beefy steak the most classic way I'm sure you will want to use a high smoke point oil coupled with butter to finish it off at the end with some 👨‍🍳 basting. Garlic and thyme are usually the two other ingredients that flavour the butter well and thus the steak.

As you can imagine that ends up being quite alot of fat! Also don't forget that the meat has it's own fat and you will want a nice piece of steak with it's own marbled fat to help with the beefy flavour, because really all the flavour is in the fat. So you will need a nice lemoney 🍋, acidic, fresh green 🥬 sauce or topping to go with this steak.

With this Salsa Verde you will find you won't need much else, not even side dishes 💚.

☑️ Ingredients

  • 1 1‚ÅÑ2 tablespoons salted capers
  • 1 cup flat-leaf or curly parsley leaves
  • 1 cup mint leaves
  • 1 cup basil leaves
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 6 anchovy fillets
  • 1 cup olive oil
  • lemon juice
  • salt and pepper

👩‍🏫 Instructions

  1. Wash the capers and soak in fresh water 🚰 for an hour.
    • Drain and squeeze dry in a tea towel.
  2. In a food processor, blend the capers, herbs, garlic and anchovies until combined.
    • Alternatively, you can use a mortar and pestle 🤓 and pound the ingredients into a paste.
  3. With the machine still running, gradually add the olive oil.
  4. When a thick paste forms, turn the machine off and stir in the lemon juice.
  5. Season 🧂 to taste, transfer to a jar or bowl and cover with a layer of oil to seal; keep, covered, for up to 7 days.

A the end of it all the sauce can last for a few days in the fridge. It is a cold sauce and should only be added to the protein at the time of eating otherwise it will cook the gentel herbs and darken the green color.

You should have a beautiful chunky emerald green looking sauce that smells intensely like cooking herbs and is very appealing to the eyes. It will taste tangy and bitter and super salty because of the capers and anchovies. It won't have any overpowering taste of fish from the anchovies, the strongest taste will be the mix of herbs.

In the end you can pick your own mix of herbs, but mint, parsley and lime are good choices.
